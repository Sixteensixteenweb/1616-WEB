SIXTEENSIXTEEN.NET

Gyimah Gariba, Khadijah Morley, Ashley Marshall

$20,00 CAD

  • 30 x 34in / Offset printing on newsprint / 2025

SIXTEENSIXTEEN.NET compiles floating thoughts, illustration, and research materials with reference to the traditional newspaper format. Separately, the contributors reflect on Black interiority, the body, gender presentation, and the social parade of normalcy. SIXTEENSIXTEEN.NET includes the works of Gyimah Gariba, Khadijah Morley, Ashley Marshall and produced with the support of Gallery 44 Centre for Contemporary Photography, Toronto Canada.
